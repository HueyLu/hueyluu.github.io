About me

I'm a software engineer and student based in New Jersey with experience building practical automation tools, websites, and database systems. I focus on creating solutions that make work faster, simpler, and more organized — from automated invoice systems to client information databases.

Through my internship at VietDanSolutions, I developed software using Python, C#, VBA, and web technologies while also redesigning and maintaining the company website. Alongside my technical work, I've taken advanced coursework in computer science, software development, project management, calculus, statistics, and economics.

Outside of engineering, I'm involved in leadership, outreach, and communication-focused roles, including Student Council Vice President and FBLA Webmaster & Head of Outreach. I bring a mix of technical skill, leadership experience, and curiosity for AI, finance, technology, and product-building.
